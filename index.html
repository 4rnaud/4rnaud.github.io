Hi! Welcome to my page!

I am a Ph.D. Student at the Economics departement (BSE) of the University of Bordeaux in France, and affiliated to the the French Institute of Pondicherry (IFP) in India, through the Observatory of Rural Dynamics and Inequalities in South India (ODRIIS). I am involved in the development of the NEEMSIS longitudinal survey since 2019.

I am currently in India until August 2023.

My main field of interest is Development Economics, and I work on topics related to household indebtedness, labour market, and cognitive skills/personality traits in rural South India, using the RUME and NEEMSIS datasets.
